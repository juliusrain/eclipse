Story

Current faster-than-light travel allows inter-planetary voyages by means of an interdimentional drive, or "IDD". With an IDD, a ship can jump from one solar system to another instantaneously. With all of its virtues, contemporary IDD technology has a limited range and accuracy.

Scientists have been researching to develop a special new engine that far exceeds anything now available. Two days ago, a prototype was tested at a secret facility in the FTL development sector. The new drive performed far better than anyone involved predicted.

Unfortunately, the test ship did so well that it overshot its target, and is now lost in a distant sector of space. This sector is inhabited by Xaxoblaxonians, who would love to get their hands on this technology. The test ship has minimal defenses. What's more, it was only equipped for a four-hour mission, with emergency supplies lasting 72 hours. It is assumed that they searched for an inhabitable planet to set down and hide.

Your ship has been ordered to the Omega-Centauri outpost on the outer edge of our territory, neighbouring that sector. Your mission is to find them, and escort them back to safety.

How to Play

+ Make sure that the right features are enabled in your browser.
Actually, in Chrome, "WebGL" must be disabled for the program to work.
Make sure that "Override software rendering list" is enabled.
Having "GPU Accelerated Drawing" enabled is a good idea, too.

Move with the mouse

Shoot by pressing the spacebar

Accelerate with the 'w' key

Hold position with the 's' key

Roll with the 'a' and 'd' keys

Press Enter to chat (in multiplayer mode)
